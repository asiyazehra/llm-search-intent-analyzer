# LLM Search Intent Analyzer 🧠

## Overview
A data-driven framework that bridges traditional search intent analysis 
with how users phrase questions to AI platforms — helping marketers 
understand the shift in user behaviour from keyword-based search 
to conversational AI queries.

As users increasingly turn to ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI 
Overviews instead of traditional search, understanding HOW they ask 
questions — and what answers AI gives — is the new competitive edge 
for content and SEO teams.

## Why This Matters
- User search behaviour is fundamentally shifting from keywords to 
  natural language questions
- Traditional keyword research tools don't capture AI query patterns
- Content optimised for Google may completely miss AI search audiences
- Brands need to understand both traditional AND conversational intent
  to stay visible

## Framework Components

### 1. 🔎 Traditional vs AI Intent Mapping
- Compares keyword data from Google Search Console with 
  conversational query patterns from AI platforms
- Identifies gaps where content ranks on Google but 
  misses AI-generated answers
- Maps intent categories: informational, navigational, 
  transactional, conversational
- Scores content for AI-readiness vs traditional SEO strength

### 2. 🧩 Query Pattern Analysis
- Clusters search queries by intent type using NLP techniques
- Identifies most common question formats used in AI platforms
  (who, what, how, why, compare, best)
- Tracks seasonal and trend shifts in query patterns
- Benchmarks brand query patterns vs competitors

### 3. 📝 Content Gap Intelligence
- Cross-references existing content against identified 
  intent clusters
- Flags high-intent topics with no content coverage
- Prioritises content opportunities by search volume 
  and AI mention potential
- Generates actionable content briefs from gap analysis

### 4. 📊 Intent-to-Conversion Funnel
- Maps search intent stages to TOFU / MOFU / BOFU funnel
- Tracks which intent types drive highest conversions
- Identifies funnel leaks caused by intent mismatch
- Recommends content adjustments per funnel stage

### 5. 📈 Performance Tracking Dashboard
- Monitors organic rankings for intent-clustered keywords
- Tracks AI platform visibility per intent category
- Measures content performance against intent targets
- Weekly reporting with trend analysis

## Key Outcomes
- Identified 30+ high-intent content gaps missed by 
  traditional keyword research
- Improved content relevance for AI search audiences 
  by aligning structure with conversational query patterns
- Reduced funnel drop-off by matching content to 
  correct intent stage
- Built reusable intent mapping framework applicable 
  across industries

## Real World Application
This framework was conceptualised from hands-on experience at 
Mailmodo — where analysing how LLMs interpret product messaging 
and tracking AI-driven referrals was a core part of the SEO 
strategy. The goal was to go beyond traditional SEO metrics and 
build a system that captures the full picture of modern search 
behaviour.
